_Magic Weapon, very rare (requires attunement)_

**Finese**  
**Reach** 10 ft

**Damage**
1d4 + 2d8 poison,
CON save DC 18 else

or [Paralyzed](. "no action/reaction, can't move, advantage to attackers, hits are crit within 5 ft") for one turn if the roll is a 1 or a 2.
